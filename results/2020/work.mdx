## 4.Work

Around 60% of Moroccan developers are employed, either in a full-time job, part-time job, or freelancers. About 28% of all participants say they're students.
Surprisingly, more than 70% of respondents reported that the Pandemic did not have a negative impact on them. On the contrary, more than 56% claim they have been more productive during the lockdown!

### What is your current role?

More than 80% of all respondents qualify themselves as developers, and more than 43% of them are full-stack developers. New emerging roles start to gain popularity in the market. Should we expect a shift in the upcoming years?

### What is your current employment status?

Almost 13% of respondents are looking for a job opportunity. Keep in mind that this survey's data was collected during the Pandemic, which impacted many jobs.

### How often do you work overtime or beyond the formal time expectation of your job?

Almost 79% of participants claim that they work at least occasionally overtime. Why is that? Is it by passion, or dedication to results, or under estimated scheduling leading to over compensation in work ?

### How do you feel about your job?

Around 80% of respondents reported they're somehow happy with their current job. This is consistent with Stackoverflow's own data on developers, feeling happy with their jobs.

### Do you work on a side project?

Almost 60% of respondents work on side projects to improve their skills, strengthen their knowledge, and invest in themselves.

More than 80% of all respondents reported that they found a job after just a couple of months of their graduation.

### What characteristics would most influence your decision to choose one job offer over another?

Salary, technical stack, and culture, are the top three motivators for Moroccan developers to consider a job offer. Data shows Industry or work impact have the less weight in career decisions.

<Chart id="work-q-6" title={false} />

### What type of company do you prefer?

More than 34% of respondents prefer to work in a multinational company.

### What is the impact of COVID-19 Pandemic in remote work policy in your company?

Moroccan IT companies promptly adopted a remote policy during the Pandemic, with more than 83% of developers working remotely.
