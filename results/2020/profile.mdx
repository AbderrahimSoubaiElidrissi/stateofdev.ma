# 2.Profile

The objective of this first section is to get insights about the profile and the skills of the people working in software engineering in Morocco. The majority of the people that answered the survey are men. Also, the majority of the respondents are junior developers with less than 3 years of experience and most of them are living in Morocco with plans to move to another country to work in the next 2 years.

### What is your gender ?

The first objective was to get the gender of the developers in the country. More than 80% of the people that filled this survey are men.

### What is your age ?

The majority of the respondents have between 20 and 24 years old which represents either junior developers or university graduates. The other category of respondents were people between 25 and 29 years old.

<Chart id="profile-q-1" title={false} />

### Where are you located ?

The majority of the answers were from people located in Morocco with 87.9% of the answers. The rest of the answers were from people located in Europe or other countries

<Chart id="profile-q-2" title={false} />

### What is your highest diploma ?

More than half of the developers that answered this question have an equivalent of a master degree. The other represented category were people that made a 2 year training after their high school degree (BAC). Some of the respondents had though a PHD degree.

<Chart id="profile-q-4" pie title={false} />

### How many years have you been coding professionally?

The majority of the responses were from people that are coding for less than a year. The second group of people are people that were coding from 1 to 3 years. Based on this data we can assume a big majority of the people who answered this survey are therefore junior developers. It's however important to note that Seniority is not only based on the numbers of years of experience

### Is coding a hobby for you?

Coding is a hobby for the majority of the respondents to this survey.

### Do you have any plans to work outside Morocco in the next 2 years?

74% of the people that answered this question have plans to work outside Morocco in the next 2 years. The rest want to stay in the country.

### If you are working abroad, do you have any plans to come back to Morocco?

More than a half of the people that are working abroad do have plans to come back to Morocco
